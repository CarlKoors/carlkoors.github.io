Programing

 Hello my name is Carl, I have been coding since the summer of 2014. It started as a hobby but it slowly evolved into an obsession. I've been around block so to speak I first started with HTML & CSS. However, it quickly evolved to: Javascript, C, C# - sharp, and C++ - plus plus. After a few years of making memory cheats for the few video games I played I switched to making discord bots. This required me to learn Python and Nodejs -- for Discord(dot)js. I eventually settled on node. I can say with certainty that “anything that can be written in javascript will be written in javascript” (FireShip).

Athletics

 Over the last few year I have had a surprising interest in athletics. Especially running, more specifically long distance running. I love the mental strain and the thrill of not knowing how, but that you will make it through the run. Which surprises a lot of people as I used to be a sprinter -- not a very good one, but still a sprinter. Anyway, I switched to distance running my sophomore year after taking up longer distance running during the winter. I can say that I love longer runs for a few reasons: the mental and physical challenges, commitment, and the feeling of completion.
